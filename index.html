The Ultimate Flow

Follow these 7 steps to master the product lifecycle.

01

Access the Dashboard

Navigate to your WordPress admin panel. On the left-hand sidebar, hover over the Products menu item to reveal the sub-commands.

Dashboard Interface Preview
02

Initialize New Product

Click on Add New. This opens the product editor where you'll define the essence of what you're selling.

+ Add New
03

Identity & Narrative

Enter a compelling Product Title and a detailed description. Remember: your description handles the selling, while the title handles the search.

04

The Data Hub

Scroll down to the Product Data widget. Here, set your price, SKU, stock levels, and shipping dimensions. Toggle between Simple and Variable products as needed.

05

Visual Excellence

Upload a high-resolution Product Image. Use the Product Gallery for alternative angles, lifestyle shots, or feature close-ups.

06

Organize for Success

Select relevant Categories and add descriptive Tags. This ensures customers can find your product through navigation and search filters.

07

The Big Launch

Review everything in the Publish box. Set your visibility settings, then hit that blue Publish button to go live worldwide.
